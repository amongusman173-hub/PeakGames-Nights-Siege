🌙 Phases

Day Phase: 60 seconds to buy gear. Shop stays open — you can keep buying until you skip or the timer runs out.

Night Phase: Zombies spawn. Kill them all to advance. Every 5 waves a Boss appears mid-wave.

☣ Infection

Infected zombies and Spitter acid raise your infection meter. Above 50% it drains HP. Use Antidote (Q) to clear it.

🪙 Perk Coins

Earned by dying at higher waves. Spend them in the Perks menu for permanent upgrades across all runs.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

Night Siege is a browser-based game. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

Night Siege does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All game progress is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.

3. Local Storage

We use localStorage to save your perk coins and purchased perks between sessions. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The Game loads fonts from Google Fonts. No other third-party services are used.

5. Cookies

Night Siege does not use cookies.

6. Contact

For questions about this policy, please refer to the game source repository.
